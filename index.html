About

Anita Al Mourany is a Lebanese Filmmaker born on August 3rd, 2000, in Aaba Al Koura north of Lebanon. She is currently finishing her Bachelor of Arts in Television and Film at the Lebanese American University in Beirut. Anita began building herself through being part of several student and professional film sets, she has worked mostly in the camera and lighting department, she was the Director of Photography of a student film “Friday Session” (2021). In addition, she has produced a student short film “Underground” and two of her own films “The Marble” (2021) and “The War Between Us” (2019). Throughout this time, Anita expanded her knowledge in theatre and was the House Manager of the Lebanon’s European Theatre Festival, in 2019. She has also gained experience by acting in an independent short film “Baal”, in 2021, and in a student play production, “كانون الثّاني 1992”, in 2021. During her time at LAU, Anita has written and directed four short films: two short fictions, “The War Between Us” (2019), and “Zahi” (2021), one short documentary “The Marble” (2021), and one short experimental “Heaven on Earth” (2021). She is currently writing her senior fiction film that will be shot in March 2022. Anita has a strong passion for photography, more specifically street and city photography that tackles the themes of chaos and post war, and reflects her country’s situation, in an unusual way.
